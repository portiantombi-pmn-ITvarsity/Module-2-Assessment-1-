GORILLAS

Gorillas are ground dwelling, predominantly herbivores apes that inhabit the forest of central sub saharan africa. The genus Gorilla into two species: the eastern gorillas and the western gorillas, and either four or five auvspecies. They are the largest living primates. DNA of gorillas is highly similar to that of humans, from 95% to 99% depending on what is included, and they are the next closest living relaives to humans after chimpanzees and bonopos.

Gorillas are cinsidered gighly intelligent. A few individuals in caotivity, such as koko; have been taught a subset of sign language. like the other great apes, gorillas can laugh, grieve, have "rich emotional lives', develop strong family bonds, make and use tools, and think about the past and future. some researchers believe gorillas have spiritual feelings or religious sentiments.
